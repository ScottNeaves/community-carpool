How It Works

SAFETY is our compulsion

and we know it's yours too!

Our 10-step safety check utilizes the latest and most rigorous safety measures available today:

  1. Driver background checks
  2. Verified Identity Assessment
  3. Driving Record Confirmation
  4. Required Vehicle Inspection
  5. Mandatory Vehicle Insurance
  6. Strict Driving Policies
  7. Cashless Transactions
  8. Viewable driver pictures and profiles
  9. Password protected pick-ups / deliveries
  10. Real-time tracking of rides

CONVENIENCE is our goal

with pre-scheduled and on-demand rides and carpools

  • Need a ride every Tuesday after school, or caught up at the office? Options exist to fit your schedule so that you can do more with your day.
  • Integrated, easy-to-use mobile app lets you choose from available drivers who you come to know with confidence.
  • Join with your neighbors to create carpools

RELIABILITY is our job

Our team and proprietary software solutions provide seamless riding experiences

  • Constant surveillance from pickup to dropoff
  • In-app reminders of dates, times and locations of pickups and dropoffs
  • Vetted, established drivers from your community
  • See driver ratings, shared friends, and driver profiles to build trust with the driver you select

COMMUNITY is our drive

Our mission is to connect neighbors through a system of shared experiences and trust, building stronger communities - one ride at a time.

Community Carpool vows to be a viable part of the change we seek and commits to give back a portion of every ride to local causes.
